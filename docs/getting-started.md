# Getting Started

Welcome to SMM Panel! This guide will help you get started with our platform.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit the [registration page](/register)
2. Enter your username, email, and password
3. Verify your email (if email confirmation is enabled)
4. Log in to your dashboard

## First Steps

### 1. Add Funds

Before placing orders, you need to add funds to your account:

1. Go to **Add Funds** in the sidebar
2. Select or enter the amount you want to add
3. Choose your payment method
4. Complete the payment
5. Enter your transaction ID
6. Wait for admin approval (usually 1-24 hours)

### 2. Browse Services

Explore available services:

1. Go to **Services** in the sidebar
2. Filter by platform (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, etc.)
3. View service details, rates, and delivery times
4. Click on a service to place an order

### 3. Place Your First Order

1. Go to **New Order** in the sidebar
2. Select a platform and category
3. Choose a service
4. Enter the link (post URL, profile URL, etc.)
5. Enter the quantity
6. Review the total charge
7. Click "Place Order"

### 4. Track Your Orders

Monitor your orders:

1. Go to **Orders** in the sidebar
2. View order status, progress, and details
3. Filter and search orders
4. Check start count and remains

## Understanding Order Status

- **Pending**: Order is waiting to be processed
- **Processing**: Order is being prepared
- **In Progress**: Order is actively being delivered
- **Completed**: Order has been fully delivered
- **Partial**: Order was partially completed
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled
- **Refunded**: Order was refunded

## Account Balance

Your balance is displayed in the sidebar and top bar. You can:
- Add funds anytime
- View transaction history
- Track spending
- Earn account points (1 point = $0.01)
